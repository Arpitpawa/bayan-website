Wellness at Bayan

Spaces to slow down and restore

Wellness at Bayan is unhurried and intuitive — shaped by stillness, simplicity and the rhythm of nature.

Across our properties, wellness unfolds quietly. From spa therapies and open-air pools to moments of solitude, each space invites balance, renewal and deep rest.

Spa Therapies

Thoughtfully designed treatments inspired by traditional practices and modern techniques for complete relaxation.

Swimming Pools

Open-air pools set within calm surroundings — ideal for slow mornings and quiet afternoons.

Mindful Movement

Gentle yoga and meditation sessions that encourage awareness, flexibility and inner calm.

Leisure Spaces

Designed for pause — lounges, gardens and quiet corners that allow the mind to wander freely.

Fitness Studio

A refined fitness environment equipped for strength, endurance and balance. Designed to complement your wellness journey while preserving the calm rhythm of your stay.

Recreation Pavilion

Thoughtfully designed indoor and outdoor leisure spaces — offering moments of play, connection and light-hearted pause for families and friends alike.
