The Udai Bagh Palace

A secluded palace retreat set amidst the tranquil hills of Udaipur.

Surrounded by nature and overlooking the Aravalli landscape, The Udai Bagh Palace offers a heritage stay defined by quiet luxury, expansive gardens, and a deep connection to its surroundings.

Rooms & Suites

Palatial spaces immersed in nature

Each room and suite reflects traditional Mewari design, offering generous space, handcrafted details, and views over gardens and surrounding hills.

Dining Experiences

Heritage-inspired cuisine served in serene settings, celebrating local flavours and seasonal ingredients.

Discover Udaipur

Explore lakes, palaces, and cultural landmarks through curated experiences rooted in local tradition.

Wellness & Stillness

Spaces designed for rest and renewal, surrounded by silence and natural beauty.

Getting here

The Udai Bagh Palace is located on the outskirts of Udaipur, approximately 40 minutes from Maharana Pratap Airport.
