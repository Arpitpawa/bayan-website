The Amargarh

A heritage-inspired retreat overlooking the timeless city of Udaipur.

Perched on a hilltop, The Amargarh blends historic character with panoramic views, offering an atmosphere of quiet sophistication and refined exclusivity.

Rooms and suites at The Amargarh reflect traditional architecture, featuring expansive layouts, natural textures, and uninterrupted views across the Udaipur landscape.
