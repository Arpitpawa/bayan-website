The Amargarh

A heritage-inspired retreat overlooking the timeless city of Udaipur.

Perched on a hilltop, The Amargarh blends historic character with panoramic views, offering an atmosphere of quiet sophistication and refined exclusivity.

Rooms & Suites

Hilltop spaces with sweeping views

Rooms and suites at The Amargarh reflect traditional architecture, featuring expansive layouts, natural textures, and uninterrupted views across the Udaipur landscape.

Dining Experiences

Enjoy regional cuisine served in elegant settings, celebrating seasonal produce and local flavours.

Explore Udaipur

Discover lakes, palaces, and cultural landmarks through curated journeys from the hilltop.

Wellness & Stillness

Spaces dedicated to quiet reflection and rejuvenation, elevated above the city.

Getting here

The Amargarh is located on a scenic hill overlooking Udaipur, approximately 45 minutes from Maharana Pratap Airport.
