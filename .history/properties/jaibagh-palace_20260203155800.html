The Jaibagh Palace

A heritage palace retreat inspired by Rajputana architecture, set amidst the quiet hills on the outskirts of Jaipur.

Designed to evoke the grandeur of a bygone era, The Jaibagh Palace offers serene courtyards, refined interiors, and an atmosphere of calm sophistication deeply rooted in place and tradition.

Rooms & Suites

Spaces shaped by quiet elegance

Each room and suite at The Jaibagh Palace is designed with an emphasis on space, light, and handcrafted detail. Soft palettes, natural materials, and views over gardens and courtyards create an atmosphere of calm retreat.

Moments in Dining

A refined culinary experience inspired by Rajasthani heritage, seasonal ingredients, and understated presentation.

A Gateway to Jaipur

Explore the cultural heart of Jaipur through curated journeys, historic landmarks, and intimate local encounters.

Wellbeing & Stillness

Spaces dedicated to rest and renewal, blending traditional wellness practices with modern comfort.

Getting here

The Jaibagh Palace is located approximately 45 minutes from Jaipur International Airport, offering a tranquil retreat within easy reach of the city.
