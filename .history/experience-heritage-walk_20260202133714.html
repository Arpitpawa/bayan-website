Heritage Walks

Culture

Walk through centuries of architecture, memory and living tradition.

Bayan’s Heritage Walks are slow explorations through palace quarters, forts, markets and forgotten pathways. Guided by local historians and storytellers, each walk reveals layers of history shaped by time, craft and daily life.

These journeys are designed to feel immersive yet unhurried, allowing space to observe details often missed.

Duration

Approximately 2–3 hours

Includes

Guided walk · Historical insights · Local narratives

Available At

Jaipur · Udaipur · Sawai Madhopur
