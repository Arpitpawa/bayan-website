Dining at Bayan

Rooted in place, season and simplicity — dining experiences shaped by Rajasthan.

Dining at Bayan reflects our connection to place and tradition. Menus are guided by seasonal produce, local flavours and a thoughtful approach to cooking — balancing regional heritage with global comfort.

Each dining space is designed to feel calm, unhurried and personal, allowing meals to unfold as experiences rather than occasions.

The Courtyard

Open-air dining set within historic surroundings, offering regional Rajasthani cuisine alongside seasonal Indian favourites.

The Dining Room

An intimate indoor space where global comfort dishes meet Indian culinary traditions, served in a calm, elegant setting.

Private Dining

Curated private dining experiences for celebrations, quiet evenings or special occasions, designed around personal preferences.

Scenic Dining

Thoughtfully selected locations across the property, offering panoramic views paired with light, seasonal menus.
