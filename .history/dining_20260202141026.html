Dining at Bayan

Rooted in place, season and simplicity — dining experiences shaped by Rajasthan.

Dining at Bayan reflects our connection to place and tradition. Menus are guided by seasonal produce, local flavours and a thoughtful approach to cooking — balancing regional heritage with global comfort.

Each dining space is designed to feel calm, unhurried and personal, allowing meals to unfold as experiences rather than occasions.

The Courtyard

Open-air dining rooted in Rajasthani culinary traditions, where meals unfold slowly beneath the open sky.

The Dining Room

An elegant indoor setting offering thoughtfully curated Indian and international dishes for relaxed dining.

Private Dining

Bespoke dining experiences created for intimate celebrations, quiet moments and meaningful gatherings.

The Viewing Gallery

A serene vantage point for sunset drinks and light bites, overlooking landscapes shaped by history and time.
