Dining Experience

The Courtyard

Open-air dining rooted in Rajasthan’s culinary heritage, The Courtyard is Bayan’s central gathering space where flavours, architecture and atmosphere come together in quiet harmony.

Set within historic stone walls, The Courtyard reflects a way of dining that is unhurried and deeply connected to place. Meals unfold slowly, accompanied by changing light, soft breezes and the rhythm of the surroundings.

Menus are inspired by regional Rajasthani recipes, prepared using seasonal produce sourced from local farmers and trusted suppliers. Traditional cooking techniques are respected, allowing each dish to express authenticity and balance.

Whether enjoyed at midday or under lantern-lit evenings, The Courtyard invites guests to pause, savour and connect — transforming dining into an experience shaped by time.
