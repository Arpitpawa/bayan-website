The Courtyard

Culture Through Cuisine

Dining at Bayan is a reflection of place, season and tradition. At The Courtyard, meals unfold within historic stone walls, celebrating Rajasthan’s culinary heritage through thoughtful, locally inspired menus prepared with quiet precision.

Ingredients are sourced from regional farms and trusted growers, allowing each dish to express authenticity and balance. Traditional techniques are respected, while subtle contemporary influences elevate the dining experience.

Whether enjoyed beneath open skies or lantern-lit evenings, The Courtyard invites guests to slow down, savour and connect — transforming dining into an experience shaped by time and place.
