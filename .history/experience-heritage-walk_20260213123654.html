Heritage Walks

Culture

Walk through centuries of architecture, memory and living tradition.

Bayan’s Heritage Walks are slow, immersive explorations through forts, palace quarters, historic streets and forgotten pathways.

Guided by local historians and storytellers, each walk reveals layers of Rajasthan’s past — shaped by craft, royalty and everyday life.

Duration

2–3 hours

Includes

Guided walk · Cultural narratives

Available At

Jaipur · Udaipur · Sawai Madhopur
