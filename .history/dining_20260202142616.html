Dining at Bayan

Culture Through Cuisine

At Bayan, dining is an expression of place and season. Our kitchens celebrate Rajasthan’s culinary heritage through thoughtfully prepared dishes, locally sourced ingredients and unhurried experiences shaped by tradition.

Menus draw inspiration from regional recipes passed through generations, prepared using seasonal produce sourced from trusted local farmers and artisans.

Whether dining beneath open skies or within intimate interiors, each experience is designed to feel calm, grounded and deeply connected to its surroundings.
