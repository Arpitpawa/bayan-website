The Unparalleled Guardian of Heritage

Guided by a deep respect for history and place, Bayan preserves the spirit of heritage while shaping spaces for contemporary living. Each destination is thoughtfully restored and quietly elevated — allowing architecture, landscape, and hospitality to exist in timeless harmony.

Hospitality, Rooted in Place

Bayan is shaped by the land it stands upon. Every experience begins with a deep understanding of local culture, history, and rhythm — allowing each space to feel grounded, authentic, and quietly refined.

A Vision Guided by Legacy

Founded under the leadership of Mr. Bhim Singh Chundawat, Bayan Group was shaped by a belief that true luxury must honour its surroundings. With years of experience in hospitality, the vision behind Bayan focuses on restoration over reinvention, and meaning over excess.

Each property reflects a thoughtful approach — preserving heritage, respecting context, and creating spaces that feel enduring rather than imposed.

A Feeling Called ‘Bayan’

At Bayan, hospitality is not performed — it is felt. Rooted in place, shaped by people, and defined by unhurried moments, every stay is designed to create connection, calm, and quiet discovery.

PAATHYA

Derived from the Sanskrit term पथ्य, meaning “the right path,” Paathya reflects Bayan’s commitment to responsible hospitality — aligned with community, culture, and conscious growth.
