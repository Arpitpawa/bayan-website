BAYAN EXPERIENCES

Celebrate Life’s Most Meaningful Moments

From intimate weddings to grand celebrations and thoughtful corporate gatherings, Bayan offers a collection of venues shaped by heritage, nature and quiet elegance.

Events at Bayan unfold slowly and intentionally. Historic courtyards, manicured lawns and refined indoor halls provide timeless settings for weddings, conferences and private celebrations. Every detail — from space planning to culinary experiences — is curated with care.

Heritage Courtyard

A dramatic open courtyard framed by centuries-old architecture, ideal for wedding ceremonies, evening receptions and cultural events.

  • Setting: Open-air heritage courtyard
  • Ideal for: Weddings · Receptions
  • Capacity: Up to 180 guests

Grand Lawns

Expansive outdoor lawns offering flexibility for large-scale celebrations, gala dinners and destination weddings under open skies.

  • Setting: Landscaped outdoor lawns
  • Ideal for: Weddings · Gala evenings
  • Capacity: Up to 300 guests

Indoor Halls & Meeting Spaces

Thoughtfully designed indoor venues suited for conferences, board meetings, workshops and private gatherings.

  • Setting: Climate-controlled indoor halls
  • Ideal for: Meetings · Conferences
  • Capacity: Customisable layouts

Private Dining & Intimate Venues

Elegant spaces for intimate celebrations, private dinners and milestone occasions, designed for privacy and warmth.

  • Setting: Indoor & outdoor private venues
  • Ideal for: Private dinners · Celebrations
  • Capacity: Up to 60 guests
