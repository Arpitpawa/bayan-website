Sunset Moments

Nature

As the day softens and light begins to change, Bayan’s landscapes reveal a quieter rhythm. Sunset Moments invite you to pause, observe and settle into the stillness that arrives with dusk.

Guided to carefully chosen viewpoints across hills, courtyards and open terraces, this experience unfolds without urgency. Golden light moves slowly across stone, water and sky while gentle breezes carry the calm of the evening.

Whether shared in silence or quiet conversation, these moments offer space for reflection and a deeper connection to place.

Duration

Approximately 2–3 hours

Best Time

Golden hour to early dusk

Inclusions

  • Guided access to scenic viewpoints
  • Light refreshments
  • Private seating arrangements
